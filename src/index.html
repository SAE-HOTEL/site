Services



Our team is available 24/7 to attend to your needs, from room service for a late-night snack to arranging your day's transport. Our concierge service excels in helping you explore the local area, offering recommendations and arranging tours to ensure you enjoy the best experiences locally. Each room is a haven of technology and comfort, featuring high-speed Wi-Fi, a state-of-the-art entertainment system, and personalized climate control for your ultimate convenience.

Rooms



Discover unparalleled comfort in our rooms, where premium mattresses, high-quality linens, and a selection of pillows await to ensure a restful sleep. Our soundproofed rooms guarantee tranquility, offering a range of options to suit every need, from single rooms to double rooms with separate beds, classic doubles, and luxurious suites. Highlighting our offerings, we have a special room dedicated to the 2024 Olympic Games, capturing the spirit of athletic achievement.

Amenities



Experience the spirit of the Olympics in our exclusive Teddy Riner-inspired room, a sanctuary of athletic excellence. For families, our Kids' Club provides a variety of fun and educational activities, ensuring entertainment for children while offering parents a chance to relax.

Experience the perfect blend of ecological responsibility and luxury at Le Cactus, centrally located in Saint-Denis.
