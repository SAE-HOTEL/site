Le Cactus

Situated at 142 Rue Gabriel Péri in the dynamic heart of Saint-Denis, Le Cactus is where traditional elegance meets modern sophistication.
Recently, we've taken significant steps to become more environmentally friendly, including upgrading our insulation for enhanced eco-efficiency. This commitment to sustainability is echoed in our eco-responsible practices, extending to the very creation of our website.
For sports enthusiasts and admirers of athletic prowess, our hotel offers a unique experience with our exclusive Olympic-themed room, celebrating the spirit of the Olympic Games.
Each stay in our thoughtfully designed rooms and suites promises comfort, luxury, and the latest technology.
Our range of amenities caters to both business and leisure travelers, featuring gourmet dining options and top-tier conference facilities.
At Le Cactus, we're not just providing a place to stay, we're offering an experience that's both indulgent and environmentally conscious, capturing the vibrant essence of Seine-Saint-Denis and creating unforgettable visits for every guest.
