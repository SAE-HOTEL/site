Single Room



Our Single Room offers a cozy and intimate setting, perfect for solo travelers. The room features a comfortable single bed, a well-appointed workspace, and a private bathroom. Designed with simplicity and functionality in mind, it includes modern amenities like high-speed Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV, and climate control, ensuring a convenient and relaxing stay.

Double Room with Separate Beds



Ideal for friends or colleagues traveling together, the Double Room with Separate Beds offers comfort and privacy. Each room is equipped with two separate beds, providing personal space and convenience. The room includes a modern entertainment system, a workspace, and an en-suite bathroom, all designed to create a comfortable and practical living space.

Classic Double Room



The Classic Double Room is a perfect blend of elegance and comfort. Designed for couples or solo travelers seeking more space, it features a plush double bed, a cozy seating area, and a private bathroom. The room is equipped with premium amenities, including a state-of-the-art entertainment system, high-speed Wi-Fi, and personalized climate control, ensuring a relaxing and enjoyable stay.

Luxurious Suites



Our Luxurious Suites are the epitome of comfort and style. These spacious suites feature a separate living area, an upscale bathroom, and a king-size bed. The refined decor and premium amenities, including a minibar, a sophisticated entertainment system, and bespoke toiletries, make these suites an ideal choice for guests seeking a higher level of luxury and convenience.

Special Room for the 2024 Olympic Games



Dedicated to the 2024 Olympic Games, this special room is a tribute to sportsmanship and athletic excellence. It boasts unique decor inspired by the Olympics, memorabilia, and an ambiance that captures the spirit of the games. The room includes top-notch amenities tailored for sports enthusiasts, offering an exclusive and immersive experience.
